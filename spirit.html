PLUM AND BLACKTHORN GIN

RHUBARB & HIBISCUS GIN

The nose on this gin is a complex menagerie of aromatics with hints of cardamom, mace and orange. Plum and blackthorn lend the gin its purplish hue, and add a fruitiness that complements the heat of black peppercorns, ginger and cinnamon. Botanicals: Juniper, coriander, green cardamom pods, black pepper, whole clove, cinnamon, mace, ginger, fresh lemon peel, fresh orange peel, orris root, almond, blackthorn berries & plum.
